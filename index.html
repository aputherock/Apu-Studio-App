About Me

The artist behind the lens

Creative Vision

My name is Apurba Goswami, and I am a Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Caravel Technical College. Alongside my academic career, I actively pursue my creative passions in photography, cinematography, and music creation, with over five years of hands-on experience in these creative fields.

I strongly believe that technology and creativity go hand in hand. My work reflects a balanced blend of technical expertise and artistic expression, allowing me to tell compelling stories through visuals and sound while maintaining a strong foundation in computer science education. Through this multidisciplinary approach, I aim to inspire both my students and creative audiences alike.
